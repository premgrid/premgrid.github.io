About Me

I'm an undergraduate student studying Mechanical Engineering at the University of Colorado Boulder.

My life revolves around the intersection of technology and art. I aspire to become a science communicator and work in policy sharing my passion for space exploration and technology with the world. I currently work in Digital Media for the CU Boulder Graduate School and the student organization, Students for the Exploration & Development of Space (SEDS).

In my spare time I enjoy creating art, landscape photography, discussing films, and advocating for science policy.
